春节喜乐会 | Lunar New Year Gala

2018春节喜乐会活动由CSSA联合AAA,TSA,HKSA,KSA,TASC主办。活动定于正月初一2月16号晚上6点至9点在YMCA举办。本次活动的目的是让所有对中国传统文化感兴趣的香槟学子有机会一起欢庆狗年春节。喜乐会门票全部免费并且采取先到先得的方式派票。喜乐会活动分为游戏区,体验区和美食区三大区块。每个区块我们都为大家准备了与往年有所不同的小惊喜！今年，有了其他兄弟组织的相助，喜乐会美食区除了提供中国传统美食之外还会有其他亚洲国家地区的传统美食出现。游戏区相比往年更加丰富，我们新增了DJ after party和Kahoot游戏，并且为Kahoot游戏的最后赢家准备了火锅大奖。参与体验区同学们将会有参与亲手制作水饺，体验中国传统书法，品茶以及参与神秘任务赢取惊喜奖励的机会 ！大家还在等什么呢？快来报名参加喜乐会吧！

The Chinese Students and Scholars Association (CSSA) is hosting the Lunar New Year Gala on February 16th from 6 to 9 PM. Our intention is to invite everyone who is interested in Chinese culture to celebrate the Year of the Dog together. This year, besides the traditional Chinese games and food, we add in a DJ after party and Kahoot game. The winner of the Kahoot game can win the prize of hotpot. The food we serve would be mainly traditional Chinese food. You would also get the chance to make dumplings yourself. In addition, you would have the opportunity to try Chinese calligraphy, taste Chinese tea and get other unique experience from Chinese culture. If you are interested in our event, further information will be posted on our CSSA Facebook page. Please keep an eye on our event. We look forward to seeing you there!
